pocnetconfschemaless documentation¶

Overview¶

pocnetconfschemaless is a proof of concept OpenDaylight application that will show you how to read and write the configuration of a NETCONF device that does not offer YANG models or whose YANG model cannot be parsed by ODL.

The poc will allow you to read and write the hostname of a variety of NETCONF devices:

  • netconf-testtool (NETCONF device simulator provided by ODL),
  • Juniper MX5 routers,
  • Nokia vSIM virtual routers.

pocnetconfschemaless has been developed at b-com. It is licensed under the Eclipse Public License v1.0.
